Our Sacred Commitment

We are a dedicated family of healers, musicians, and guides united by a profound respect for ancestral traditions and the sacred medicines that connect us to spirit.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to create a safe, supportive, and sacred space for deep healing and spiritual exploration. We honor the indigenous lineages from which these practices originate, particularly the Shuar tradition, and are committed to preserving their integrity and wisdom.

  • Authenticity: Upholding the traditional practices and teachings with utmost respect.
  • Safety: Ensuring the physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being of all participants.
  • Integration: Providing tools and support to help you carry the lessons of your journey into your daily life.
