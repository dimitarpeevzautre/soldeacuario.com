Our Medicine Retreats

A profound journey of healing, purification, and connection, guided by sacred plant medicines and ancient rituals.

Kambô

The sacred frog secretion for deep physical and energetic cleansing. We honor its Amazonian origins, applying it with intention and care to purify the body and strengthen the spirit.

Rapé

A sacred shamanic snuff from the Amazon, used to clear the mind, open the third eye, and ground the spirit. Served with deep respect for its traditional use in prayer and meditation.

Tabaco

A grounding master plant, used ceremonially to cleanse, protect, and carry our prayers. Its ritualistic importance lies in its power to connect us to the earth and the present moment.

Vision Quest

A sacred ceremony of seeking vision, guidance, and connection with the spirit world through solitude, prayer, and communion with nature.

Ceremonial Tools

Kuripes & Tepis

Sacred pipes for administering Rapé. High-quality, ethically sourced shamanic tools available for purchase during the retreat.

Ceremonial Drum

The voice of the Great Spirit. Used to move energy, induce trance states, and call in protective spirits.

Chakapa

A rattle made of bundled leaves, used to cleanse a person's energetic field with rhythmic sound.
